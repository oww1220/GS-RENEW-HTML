Environmental Management

Guidelines for Environmental Management Goals & Activities of Environmental Management Achievements of Environmental Management

Guidelines for Environment by GS Global to Promote Sustainable Management

01
The senior management ought to consider the environmental impacts during decision making.
02
The Company shall comply with environment-related laws and regulations,
and shall prepare and completely fulfill the strict internal standards.
03
The Company shall invest utmost efforts to save resources, and shall continuously engage in activities for improvement.

Goals of Environmental Management

Campaign Activities of GS Global to Save Resources

01
Separate collection of general wastes from recyclable materials (paper, plastic, bottle)
02
Automatic power-off during recess hours and nighttime to save electric power
03
“Save Water Campaign” at restrooms to reduce the use of water

GS Global continues to carry out the activities to decrease the wastes discharged as well as the use of electric power and water, by implementing environmental management.

- The above figures are the proportioned values from the ratio of usable area compared to the annual use at GS Tower.
- The volume of wastes discharged has been measured based on inflammable wastes excluding recyclable materials.
