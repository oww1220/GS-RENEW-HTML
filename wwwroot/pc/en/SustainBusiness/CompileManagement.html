Compliance Management

Fair Trade Anti-Corruption
  • GS Global currently operates the following systems in order to comply with fair trading practices
    • Operation of fair trade programs
    • Designation of an officer in charge of fair trade (an autonomous compliance officer)
    • Operation of a reporting channel against violation of fair trading for partner companies
    • Preparation of a self-compliance manual and implementation of training regarding fair trade for executives and employees
Regulations regarding Operation of Fair Trade Programs (PDF) Autonomous Compliance Manual (PDF)
  • GS Global currently operates the following systems to prevent corruptions
    • Activities to enhance compliance through a compliance volunteer system
    • Implementation of internal audit by a monitoring body and operation of an internal accounting management system
    • Assured systems for anonymous reporting and protection of reporters’ identity
    • Training regarding anti-corruption oriented to executives and employees
