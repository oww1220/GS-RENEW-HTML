Charter of Corporate Governance

  • GS Global (hereinafter referred to as “Company”) shall endeavor to promote sustainable growth and create new life values for better tomorrow together with customers.
  • As a solid foundation of management activities for sustainable growth, the Company shall enact the “GS Global Charter of Corporate Governance” and aims to solidify and further advance the sound corporate governance by forming the board of directors and audit organization equipped with expertise and independence.
  • Moreover, the Company shall build complete reliability with various stakeholders, including customers, shareholders and the society, through firm establishment of sound corporate governance, and shall dedicate itself to attaining mutual growth.
